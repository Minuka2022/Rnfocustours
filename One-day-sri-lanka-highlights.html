One Day Sri lanka Highlights

A day of culture, history, and scenic beauty in Sri Lanka.

Overview

Experience the best of Sri Lanka in a single day with this action-packed tour. Explore Negombo’s vibrant fish markets, ascend the iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress, and enjoy a scenic drive through picturesque landscapes. Savor authentic Sri Lankan cuisine, visit a tea plantation, and end the day with a serene sunset stroll along Negombo Beach. Perfect for those seeking a quick yet immersive cultural and historical experience.

Itinerary

Day 1

Start your day exploring Negombo’s bustling fish markets. Embark on a scenic drive to Sigiriya, where you’ll climb the iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress. Enjoy a traditional Sri Lankan lunch, visit a tea plantation, and return to Negombo for a sunset stroll along the beach.
