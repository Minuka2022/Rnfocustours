Luxury Honeymoon

Transfer to Colombo Airport with cherished memories.

Overview

Indulge in a luxurious 7-day honeymoon across Sri Lanka’s top destinations. From the misty hills of Nuwara Eliya to the serene beaches of Bentota, this tour offers the perfect blend of romance, adventure, and relaxation. Enjoy private tours, luxury stays, and personalized experiences, creating unforgettable memories with your loved one.

Itinerary

Day 1

Visit the Elephant Orphanage, enjoy lunch in Kandy, and admire Ramboda Waterfall.

Day 2

Explore Little England, Gregory Park, and the New Zealand farm.

Day 3

Visit Dunhinda Waterfall and relax in luxury accommodations.

Day 4

Explore the Nine Arch Bridge, Little Adam’s Peak, and Rawana Waterfall.

Day 5

Travel to Bentota via Yala and Udawalawe National Parks.

Day 6

Enjoy whale watching, visit Galle Fort, and relax on the beach.

Day 7

Transfer to Colombo Airport with unforgettable memories.
